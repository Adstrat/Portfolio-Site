Mission Statement

I've always enjoyed creativity, in a structured kind of way. Problem solving, making things fit, or work well, is somehow rewarding. I like that sense of completion, when things come together, to flow beautifully.

I think in this modern, technological world, there's a chance to make an impact. The internet is a mega powerful tool, which can be harnessed for great things. It is my hope that I can be a part of that.

My Background

Born in Brighton, England, I've tried my hand at a few things. With an interest in the mind, I made a diploma in hypnotherapy in 2003, which led me to explore meditation and yoga. Some years later I pursued a yearning to make things out of wood, to build in a very physical way, and studied carpentry. Throughout this time I worked in social care, mainly with people with autism and learning disabilities.

A few years ago I moved to Berlin with my family and decided to pursue web development, out of a desire to be involved in helping to steer technology in a beneficial direction.

Aside from programming, I also enjoy juggling, painting and being in nature.
